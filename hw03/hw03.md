### Look at the spread of GDP per capita within the continents.

``` r
min_max = gapminder %>% 
  group_by(continent) %>% 
  summarise(maxGDP = max(gdpPercap), 
            minGDP = min(gdpPercap),
            meanGDP = mean(gdpPercap),
            sdGDP = sd(gdpPercap))

knitr::kable(min_max, col.names = c("Continent", "Max GDP", "Min GDP", "Mean GDP", ""))
```
